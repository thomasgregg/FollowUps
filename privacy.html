FollowUps

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

FollowUps helps you record a conversation, turn it into follow-up tasks, and optionally create Apple Reminders from those tasks. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

How FollowUps works

  • FollowUps records audio only after you explicitly start recording.
  • When you stop recording, the app sends the saved audio to OpenAI for transcription and task extraction.
  • The app stores sessions, transcripts, tasks, and recordings locally on your device unless you delete them.
  • Apple Reminders are created only when you explicitly choose selected tasks and confirm.

Data we handle

  • Audio recordings you choose to capture.
  • Transcript and task content generated from that recording.
  • Reminder data only when you choose to create Apple Reminders.

How we use data

  • To transcribe recorded audio.
  • To generate follow-up tasks and a short session headline.
  • To let you review, edit, save, and optionally export tasks to Apple Reminders.
  • To store sessions locally so you can revisit them later.

Third-party processing

After recording stops, FollowUps sends the recorded audio and related transcript content to OpenAI so OpenAI can transcribe the conversation and extract tasks. OpenAI processes this data according to its own policies. You can review OpenAI's privacy information at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

Data sharing

FollowUps does not sell your data and does not use your data for advertising or tracking. Data is shared with OpenAI only to provide transcription and task extraction, and with Apple Reminders only when you choose to create reminders.

Storage and deletion

  • Sessions and recordings are stored locally on your device.
  • You can delete individual sessions, which also deletes the associated recording.
  • You can delete all sessions from within the app.
  • You can optionally enable automatic cleanup for older sessions in Settings.

Permissions

  • Microphone: required to record audio.
  • Reminders: required only if you choose to create Apple Reminders.
  • Notifications: optional, used to notify you when processing finishes in the background.

Children's privacy

FollowUps is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at this page.

Contact

For privacy questions about FollowUps, please use the project contact channel at github.com/thomasgregg/FollowUps/issues.

FollowUps does not monitor or intercept phone calls or audio from other apps. Recording happens only when you explicitly start it.
